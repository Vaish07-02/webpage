Veg Biryani

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps

Step 1: Prepare Rice
Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it in water for about 20 minutes before cooking.
Step 2: Cook the Rice
Boil water in a pot, add a pinch of salt, and cook the rice until it is partially cooked.
Step 3: Cook Vegetables
Heat oil in a pan and fry chopped onions, ginger, garlic and vegetables until soft.
Step 4: Add Spices
Add spices such as turmeric, garam masala and chili powder to enhance the flavor.
Step 5: Mix Rice
Add the cooked rice to the vegetable mixture and gently mix everything together.
Step 6: Final Cooking
Cover the pan and cook for 10 minutes on low heat so that the flavors combine well.

Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Cheese

Steps

Step 1: Boil Water
Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil.
Step 2: Cook Pasta
Add pasta to the boiling water and cook it for about 8–10 minutes until soft.
Step 3: Drain Pasta
Drain the cooked pasta using a strainer and rinse it with cold water.
Step 4: Prepare Sauce
Heat oil in a pan and add garlic and tomato sauce.
Step 5: Mix Pasta
Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix it well.
Step 6: Add Cheese
Sprinkle grated cheese and herbs for extra taste and serve hot.

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables

Steps

Step 1: Prepare Pizza Base
Take a pizza base and place it on a baking tray.
Step 2: Add Sauce
Spread tomato pizza sauce evenly across the pizza base.
Step 3: Add Toppings
Add vegetables such as onions, capsicum, olives and mushrooms.
Step 4: Add Cheese
Sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
Step 5: Bake Pizza
Place the pizza in a preheated oven at 180°C.
Step 6: Serve
Bake for about 10–15 minutes until the cheese melts and the crust becomes crispy.
